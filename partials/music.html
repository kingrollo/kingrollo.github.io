Solo

I made a couple of albums of instrumental electronic music a very long time ago. My current project is remastering these and getting them back out into the wild, with companion albums containing bonus tracks and previously unreleased material. You can find more information about them by clicking on the cover pictures below.

Duo

I'm also half of a synthpop duo called Hypernova. We haven't done an awful lot recently, but still have plans for world domination.

Blog

I also run a blog called Music for Stowaways, which I started in 2012 in an attempt to keep my knowledge of music "current". It no longer is, but the blog soldiers on...
